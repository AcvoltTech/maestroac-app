1. Introduction

ACVOLT Tech School Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use MaestroAC ("App" or "Service").

This policy applies to all users of our App, including students, technicians, and visitors.

Please read this policy carefully. By using our App, you consent to the practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

Account Information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City and State
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Professional Information:

  • Certification status (EPA 608, NATE, OSHA, ESCO)
  • Years of experience
  • Specialization areas

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our App, we automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • IP address
  • App usage data (features used, time spent, screens viewed)
  • Quiz and study progress
  • Attendance records (check-in/check-out times)
  • Study session duration

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • ACVOLT Tech School enrollment records (for enrolled students)
  • Payment processors (Stripe) - transaction confirmation only
  • Authentication providers (for OTP/Magic Link services)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: Creating accounts, providing access to content, tracking progress
  • Educational Purposes: Personalizing study recommendations, tracking completion
  • Communications: Account updates, support, educational reminders
  • Attendance Tracking: Recording study time for enrolled students
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage, fixing bugs, developing features
  • Security: Detecting fraud, preventing unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

ACVOLT Tech School Inc. does NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase), email services (Resend), payment processing (Stripe)
  • ACVOLT Tech School: Progress reports for enrolled students
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase, located in the United States.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Secure authentication (password hashing, OTP)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

5.3 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data removed within 30 days of deletion request
  • Backup Data: Removed within 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 All Users

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6.2 California Residents (CCPA Rights)

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights:

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of personal information collected, used, and shared
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

6.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your privacy rights, contact us:

  • Email: soporte@maestromario.com
  • Subject line: "Privacy Request - [Your Request Type]"

We will respond within 45 days as required by California law.

7. Children's Privacy

MaestroAC is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our App uses:

  • Local Storage: To save your preferences and progress locally
  • Session Data: To maintain your login session
  • Analytics: To understand App usage (no personally identifiable information)
